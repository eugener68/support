EbookLib

OPDS eBook Catalog Browser and Library

About EbookLib

EbookLib is a powerful and modern iOS application that allows users to browse and manage OPDS eBook catalogs, download books in multiple formats, and enjoy a seamless reading experience across devices. With iCloud integration, your catalog list and downloaded books stay in sync between your iPhone and iPad.

Key Features

  • Add, edit, and delete OPDS catalogs
  • Browse OPDS catalogs from pre-set lists or web OPDS list
  • Browse and search eBooks within added catalogs
  • User catalog list is stored in iCloud and shared across devices
  • Download eBooks in preferred formats
  • New Library tab to view and organize downloaded books
  • Open downloaded EPUB books in the application's built-in reader
  • Open FB2 books in application of your preference (e.g., KyBook3, eBoox etc)

Get Started

Download EbookLib from the Apple App Store and start exploring a world of free eBooks today!
