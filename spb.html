Simple Playlist Maker

Auto-generate playlists for Spotify from your artist list

About Playlist Maker

Playlist Maker is an iOS app that creates playlists from a user-provided list of artists. These playlists could be imported to user's personal Spotify account You can type artist names manually, paste a list from the clipboard, or import artists from CSV or TXT files. The app helps you control variety and structure, so your playlist feels balanced instead of repetitive.

Key Features

  • Create playlists from an artist list (manual entry, paste, or CSV/TXT import)
  • Set the total number of tracks
  • Limit tracks per artist to keep a good mix
  • Shuffle the playlist for variety
  • Prefer original versions when available
  • Auto-generate playlist names with an optional current date
  • Dry Run mode: preview the track list without creating a playlist on Spotify
  • Localization: the app follows your iPhone language (Russian supported)

How It Works

First, sign in to your Spotify account. Then choose your playlist settings, enter a playlist name (optionally with today’s date), and run a Dry Run to review the generated track list. If everything looks right, create the playlist in Spotify and start listening.

Privacy

Your Spotify account data is not stored or shared by the app. Authentication is used only to create playlists in your own Spotify account.
